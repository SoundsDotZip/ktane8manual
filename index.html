wires

cut the correct wire based on these conditions


  • 3 wires

    • 1. no red: 2nd wire

    2. 1 or more blue: 2nd blue wire

    3. otherwise: last wire


  • 4 wires

    • 1. 1 or more red and serial last digit odd: last red wire

    2. no red and last wire is yellow: 1st wire

    3. 1 blue: 1st wire

    4. 2 or more yellow: last wire

    5. otherwise: 2nd wire


  • 5 wires

    • 1. last wire is black and serial last digit odd: 4th wire

    2. 1 red and 1 or more yellow: 1st wire

    3. no black: 2nd wire

    4. otherwise: 1st wire


  • 6 wires

    • 1. no yellow and serial last digit odd: 3rd wire

    2. 1 yellow and 1 or more white: 4th wire

    3. no red: last wire

    4. otherwise: 4th wire



    button

    press or hold the button based on these conditions


    1. blue and "abort" = hold

    2. "boom" and more than 1 batteries = quick press

    3. white and lit indicator CAR = hold

    4. more than 2 batteries and lit indicator FRK = quick press

    5. yellow = hold

    6. red and "hold" = quick press

    7. else = hold


    releasing a held button

    blue = timer has a 4 in it

    yellow = timer has a 5 in it

    white / red = timer has a 1 in it



    keypads

    find all 4 symbols in 1 column and press them in order from top to bottom
